Preguntas frecuentes

What is the best free alternative to Notion?+
Anytype and Obsidian are the best free alternatives. Anytype is closest in concept (blocks, databases) but with local encryption. Obsidian is ideal if you prefer working with local Markdown files.
Is there an open source alternative to Notion?+
Yes. Anytype and AppFlowy are open source. Anytype is more mature and offers a similar experience with blocks and databases, while AppFlowy is growing rapidly.
Is Notion worth paying for or are there better alternatives?+
It depends on your use. If you only need notes and documents, Obsidian is better and free. If you need project management along with docs, ClickUp offers more features at a similar price.
Can I easily migrate from Notion to another tool?+
Notion allows export in Markdown, CSV and HTML. Most alternatives like Obsidian and Anytype accept Markdown import, making migration easier.
Which Notion alternative works offline?+
Obsidian and Anytype work perfectly offline. Obsidian stores everything in local Markdown files. Anytype has encrypted P2P sync that works without connection.
