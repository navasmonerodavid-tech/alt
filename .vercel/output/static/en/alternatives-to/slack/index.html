Preguntas frecuentes

What is the best free alternative to Slack?+
Discord is the most complete free alternative, with unlimited voice, text and video channels. For professional teams, Mattermost and Rocket.Chat have free self-hosted versions.
Is there an open source alternative to Slack?+
Yes. Mattermost, Rocket.Chat and Zulip are open source and can be installed on your own server. Mattermost is closest to Slack in user experience.
